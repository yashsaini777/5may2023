First Kejriwal, then Nitish’: BJP leader jabs INDIA as big names skip meets

BJP leader Dushyant Kumar Gautam said, “I believe their (INDIA Bloc) alliance plan will fail soon… who knows how many will skip the third one.”

National General Secretary of Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) Dushyant Kumar Gautam, taking a jibe at the Opposition's INDIA alliance, said on Friday that any alliance by opposition parties would not work anywhere.
